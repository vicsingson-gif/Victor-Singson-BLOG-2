Enderun Experience

Reflections, learnings, and unforgettable moments.

The Enderun Experience taught me a lot of things beyond the four corners of the classroom. A lot of topics that could help me in my daily life as a student as well as my future endeavor and as a public servant. It gave me the opportunity to learn valuable lessons that cannot always be found in books or lectures. Throughout the experience, I was able to learn more about businesses, teamwork, and leadership. I also learned the importance of discipline, responsibility, and time management in accomplishing tasks effectively.

The activities and discussions helped me become more aware of the different challenges faced by communities and how businesses work nowadays featuring newest trends. I realized that serving the people requires patience, dedication, honesty, and compassion. The experience also inspired me to become more active and financially aware.

Moreover, the Enderun Experience allowed me to meet new people, build friendships, and share ideas with others who have different perspectives in life. These meaningful experiences motivated me to strive harder in my studies and prepare myself for future responsibilities. Overall, the Enderun Experience became an unforgettable journey that shaped my character and inspired me to become a better individual and future public servant.

One of the seminars I attended that made the greatest impact on me was the “Surfs Up to Success” seminar held on May 06, 2026. This talk featured some of the country’s finest companies such as Universal Robina Corporation, Raffles Makati, and Fairmont Makati, which shared their inspiring success stories, business journeys, and the ways they successfully built their brands. Listening to their experiences motivated me to work harder and believe that success can be achieved through perseverance, dedication, and passion.

What made the seminar meaningful to me was the valuable lessons shared by the speakers about leadership, innovation, customer service, and professionalism. They explained that building a successful company does not happen overnight because it requires commitment, patience, and continuous improvement. I was inspired by how these companies overcame challenges and maintained their reputation in their respective industries.

The seminar also taught me the importance of having a clear vision and strong determination in achieving my goals. As a student and future public servant, I realized that success is not only about personal achievements but also about making a positive impact on other people’s lives.

I actively participated in each of these memorable activities by attending the seminars and talks with enthusiasm, interest, and an open mind. Participating in these events gave me motivation to continue striving hard in my studies and to pursue my personal goals in life. I always looked forward to joining these seminars because they provided me with valuable knowledge, inspiration, and opportunities to learn from experienced speakers and professionals.

During the activities, I listened carefully to the discussions and took down important notes that I could apply in my academic journey and future career. I also participated by asking questions, sharing my thoughts, and engaging in conversations with my fellow students and the guest speakers. These interactions helped me gain confidence in expressing my ideas and opinions while also learning from the perspectives of others.

Moreover, I actively cooperated in group activities and discussions whenever there were collaborative tasks during the seminars. I enjoyed exchanging ideas and experiences with other participants because it allowed me to broaden my understanding of different topics. Through my active participation, I realized the importance of communication, teamwork, and continuous learning. Overall, these memorable activities became meaningful experiences that inspired me to become more motivated, responsible, and goal-oriented in both my studies and personal life.

The different seminars and talks such as “Building a Career in Global Finance,” “Surfs Up to Success,” “The Better You,” and the “Overview Internship Seminar” provided me with valuable insights and important life skills that can help me in both my studies and future career. These activities taught me the importance of professionalism, confidence, communication, and continuous self-improvement in achieving success.

The seminar about building a career in global finance helped me understand the significance of financial literacy, proper decision-making, and career planning. It also inspired me to become more responsible in managing resources and preparing for future opportunities. Meanwhile, “Surfs Up to Success” taught me lessons about perseverance, leadership, innovation, and how successful companies build strong reputations through hard work and dedication.

“The Better You” seminar encouraged me to improve myself personally and emotionally by developing self-confidence, discipline, and a positive mindset. On the other hand, the “Overview Internship Seminar” prepared me for real-world experiences by teaching workplace ethics, teamwork, adaptability, and professionalism.

Overall, these seminars and talks enhanced my knowledge, strengthened my interpersonal skills, and motivated me to become more determined in reaching my goals. They also prepared me to face future challenges as a student, professional, and responsible public servant.

My overall takeaway from my time in Enderun is that learning goes beyond academics and classroom discussions. The experience helped me grow not only as a student but also as an individual who is preparing for future responsibilities and challenges. Through the different seminars, activities, and interactions with speakers and fellow students, I gained valuable knowledge, practical skills, and life lessons that I can apply in my personal and professional life.

One of the most important lessons I learned is the value of discipline, hard work, and perseverance in achieving success. The talks and seminars inspired me to become more confident, goal-oriented, and determined to pursue my dreams despite the challenges that may come along the way. I also realized the importance of communication, teamwork, leadership, and professionalism in building good relationships and becoming effective in any field or career.

Moreover, my time in Enderun encouraged me to become more open-minded and eager to continuously improve myself. It motivated me to become more responsible and prepared for future opportunities, especially in serving others as a future public servant. Overall, the Enderun experience became a meaningful and unforgettable journey that shaped my character, broadened my perspective, and inspired me to strive for excellence in everything I do.
