About Me

I firmly believe that leadership begins with service, compassion, and a genuine commitment to improving the lives of others. My experiences in youth development, public engagement, and community activities have shaped my values and strengthened my dedication to public service.

Throughout my journey, I have learned the importance of discipline, responsibility, teamwork, and perseverance. Every challenge and opportunity has contributed to my personal growth and inspired me to continue striving for excellence in everything that I do.

I am passionate about empowering young people, promoting meaningful initiatives, and creating positive change within communities. Through continuous learning and collaboration, I aspire to become a leader who serves with integrity, humility, and compassion.

This website reflects my experiences, advocacies, memorable activities, and personal reflections that continue to shape my character and future aspirations.

10 Years from now

There’s a saying, “Live while you’re young,” which is about enjoying life and making the most of the present. Entering my mid-20s is exciting, but it also makes me think about my future. As a student and, at the same time, a public servant, I see myself ten years from now as a leader whom people can rely on, a leader who always seeks the welfare of my constituents.

Ten years from now, I want to see myself serving a community, doing my best to provide the help and services that people deserve. The responsibility I have now to lead my fellow youth is one of the foundations that will help me become the leader I aspire to be in the future. It has always been my dream to lead a community where I can work closely with the people and serve them wholeheartedly.

To my future self ten years from now, I hope you are enjoying life while being responsible enough to fulfill your duties. I want you to know that your younger self from ten years ago is proud of where you are today. I hope you have touched many people’s lives and never grow tired of doing what you love most, leading and serving others.

I also want you to remember that if you make mistakes, it’s okay because they are part of your learning journey. I hope you are braver now than you were before. I wish you nothing but the best.

30 Years from now

Every milestone in our lives should be celebrated, no matter what we have achieved. Thinking about your future 30 years from now may seem too far ahead, but for me, thinking about it now gives me the determination to keep doing my best in everything I do. Thirty years from now, I see myself as a successful leader in our community, a responsible father, and a loving husband.

As a successful leader 30 years from now, I hope I will have provided my community with the services and support they need. I also hope to see people united through my leadership and the youth actively engaging in leadership as well. Thirty years from now, I wish to be a leader who has helped build a peaceful community where people are not afraid of their leaders and feel comfortable expressing their needs and concerns. At 54 years old, before I pass the responsibility of leadership to the younger generation, I hope I will have left behind a legacy they will never forget.

I also hope that 30 years from now, I will have established a successful business that provides for my family. I want it to be a business that creates jobs in our community and offers fair compensation that truly reflects the value of people’s work.

To my future self 30 years from now, I hope you enjoyed your years of serving the community. I just want to tell you that making it this far is what truly defines your success. I hope that leading others while building your own family has brought you happiness and fulfillment. In the years ahead, you may decide to retire from leadership, but I hope you continue to inspire others through the legacy you have built in your community.

As the head of your family, I hope you were able to give them not only your support but also your time and presence, so they always felt your love. I know you can achieve all of these dreams, and that is why I am already so proud of you.
